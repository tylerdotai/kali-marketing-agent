# Email Nurture Sequence

## Overview
Pre-built email sequences for lead nurturing and campaign follow-up.

## Trigger Phrases
- "email sequence"
- "nurture sequence"
- "follow up emails"
- "newsletter sequence"
- "welcome series"

## Sequence Types

### 1. Welcome Sequence (5 Emails)
For new subscribers/contacts

```
Email 1 (Day 0 - Immediate):
Subject: Welcome! Here's where to start...
Body: Brief intro, what they'll get, one valuable resource

Email 2 (Day 2):
Subject: Our story / Why we do what we do
Body: Authenticity, brand story, build trust

Email 3 (Day 5):
Subject: Our best [topic] tips
Body: Value-first, educational content

Email 4 (Day 7):
Subject: What makes us different
Body: Unique value proposition, social proof

Email 5 (Day 10):
Subject: Ready to [goal]?
Body: Soft CTA, ask for engagement
```

### 2. Product Launch Sequence (7 Emails)

```
Email 1 (10 days before): Preview/Teaser
Email 2 (7 days before): Behind the scenes
Email 3 (3 days before): Last chance preview
Email 4 (1 day before): Anticipation build
Email 5 (Launch day): THE ANNOUNCEMENT
Email 6 (3 days after): Social proof/fomo
Email 7 (7 days after): Limited time offer
```

### 3. Abandoned Cart Sequence (3 Emails)
For e-commerce

```
Email 1 (1 hour later): "Did you forget something?"
Email 2 (24 hours later): "Still thinking about it?"
Email 3 (72 hours later): "Last chance" + discount offer
```

## Subject Line Formulas

### Attention Grabbers
- "[Number] ways to [benefit]"
- "The truth about [topic]"
- "We need to talk about [topic]"
- "[Name], your [problem] isn't your fault"

### Curiosity
- "What happened next will surprise you..."
- "The [industry] secret nobody talks about"
- "We made a mistake..."

### Urgency
- "Last chance: [offer] ends [date]"
- "Only [X] spots left..."
- "Tonight only: [offer]"

## Open Rate Optimization
- Keep subject lines under 50 characters
- Personalize with name: "[First Name],"
- Use questions: "Ready for [benefit]?"
- Emojis work (use sparingly): 🔥 👇 ✨

## Template Variables
