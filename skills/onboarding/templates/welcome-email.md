# Welcome Email Template

**Subject:** Welcome to SaltHaus, [Client Name]! Let's Get Started 🚀

---

Dear [First Name],

Welcome! I'm thrilled you've chosen SaltHaus to help [Client Company] achieve [their goal].

Over the next [X] months, we'll be working together to [brief description of work].

**Here's what happens next:**

1. **Kickoff Call** — Within the next few days, I'll reach out to schedule our kickoff meeting where we'll dive into your goals, timeline, and how we like to work together.

2. **Documents** — To hit the ground running, I'll send over a quick checklist of materials I need from you (usually takes 15 minutes to gather).

3. **Strategy** — After our kickoff, I'll put together a customized plan based on what I learn about your business.

**What I need from you right now:**
- A calendar link so we can find time for our kickoff
- The best email/phone to reach you

**Quick question** — what's the ONE thing you most want to accomplish working together?

Looking forward to it!

Best,
Kali

---
Kali O'Campo
SaltHaus Group
[Phone]
[Email]

---

## Variables

| Variable | Replace With |
|----------|--------------|
| `[First Name]` | Client's first name |
| `[Client Name]` | Client's full name |
| `[Client Company]` | Their company name |
| `[their goal]` | What they hired you for |
| `[X months]` | Engagement length |
| `[brief description]` | Type of work |

## Tips

- Send within 24 hours of signing
- Keep it warm but professional
- Don't overload with info — save details for kickoff
- Ask one simple question to start the conversation
