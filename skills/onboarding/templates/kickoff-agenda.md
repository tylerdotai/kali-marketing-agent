# Kickoff Meeting Agenda

**Client:** [Client Name]
**Company:** [Client Company]
**Date:** [Date]
**Duration:** 60 minutes

---

## Agenda

### 1. Welcome & Introductions (5 min)
- Thank them for choosing SaltHaus
- Quick introductions (who's on their team)
- How we like to communicate

### 2. Goals & Priorities (15 min)
- What success looks like for them
- Top 3 priorities for the engagement
- Any quick wins they want first

### 3. Current State (10 min)
- What's currently working
- What's not working
- Current marketing channels/tools

### 4. Audience & Market (10 min)
- Who are their customers
- What makes them different
- Main competitors

### 5. Timeline & Milestones (10 min)
- Key dates/deadlines
- Reporting cadence
- When they want to see results

### 6. Next Steps (5 min)
- Action items from today
- Documents needed
- Schedule next call

### 7. Open Questions (5 min)
- Anything they want to ask

---

## Pre-Meeting Checklist

- [ ] Send calendar invite with agenda
- [ ] Prepare client background research
- [ ] Pull up any preliminary ideas
- [ ] Confirm tech setup (video/phone)

## Post-Meeting Actions

- [ ] Send meeting notes summary
- [ ] Create Asana/Trello project (if using)
- [ ] Set 30-60-90 day milestones
- [ ] Send document collection checklist
- [ ] Schedule next check-in
