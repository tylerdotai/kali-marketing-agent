# Content Calendar Generator

## Usage
When user says "create content calendar" or "plan posts for [topic]", generate a 4-week content calendar.

## Output Format

```
## Content Calendar: [Campaign/Topic]

### Week 1: [Theme]
| Day | Platform | Content Type | Caption/Notes |
|-----|----------|--------------|---------------|
| Mon | Instagram | [Carousel] | [Hook + CTA] |
| Wed | Facebook | [Video] | [Story angle] |
| Fri | Instagram | [Single Image] | [Engagement question] |

### Week 2: [Theme]
[...same format...]

### Best Times to Post
- Instagram: 9 AM - 11 AM, 7 PM - 9 PM
- Facebook: 10 AM - 12 PM
- TikTok: 7 PM - 10 PM

### Content Mix Recommendation
- 40% Value/Education
- 30% Entertainment/Behind scenes
- 20% Social proof/Testimonials
- 10% Promotional/CTA
```

## Hashtag Strategy
- 3-5 broad hashtags (1M+ posts)
- 5-10 niche hashtags (10K-500K posts)
- 2-3 campaign-specific hashtags
