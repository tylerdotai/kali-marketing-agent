# Campaign Brief Template

Use this template when creating a new marketing campaign:

---

## Campaign Brief

### 1. Campaign Name
*What are we calling this?*

### 2. Campaign Objective
*What is the one thing we want to achieve?*
- [ ] Brand awareness
- [ ] Lead generation
- [ ] Sales/conversions
- [ ] Community building
- [ ] Product launch
- [ ] Other: ____________

### 3. Target Audience
*Who are we talking to?*
- **Demographics:** Age, location, income, etc.
- **Psychographics:** Values, interests, lifestyle
- **Pain points:** What problems do they have?
- **Goals:** What are they trying to achieve?

### 4. Key Message
*What do we want them to remember/feel/do?*

### 5. Channels
*Where will we reach them?*
- [ ] Instagram
- [ ] Facebook
- [ ] TikTok
- [ ] Email Newsletter
- [ ] Website/Blog
- [ ] Press/Media
- [ ] Other: ____________

### 6. Campaign Timeline
- **Start Date:** __________
- **Launch Date:** __________
- **End Date:** __________

### 7. Budget
*Any constraints on paid promotion?*

### 8. Success Metrics
*How will we know it worked?*
- Reach: __________
- Engagement rate: __________
- Conversions: __________
- Revenue: __________

### 9. Assets Needed
*What creative do we need?*
- Social graphics: ___
- Videos: ___
- Email templates: ___
- Landing page: ___

### 10. Competitors
*Who else is targeting this audience?*
- ____________

---

## Agent Output Format

When user asks to "create campaign" or "plan launch", output:

```markdown
## Campaign: [Name]

### Objective
[One sentence goal]

### Target Audience
[3-5 bullet points]

### Key Message
[Core message in 1-2 sentences]

### Timeline
| Phase | Dates | Activities |
|-------|-------|------------|
| Pre-Launch | Week 1-2 | [List] |
| Soft Launch | Week 3 | [List] |
| Launch | Week 4 | [List] |

### Content Calendar
[First 2 weeks of posts]

### Required Assets
- [ ] [Asset 1]
- [ ] [Asset 2]

### Reminders to Set
[Apple Reminder tasks with due dates]

### KPIs
[3-5 metrics to track]
```
